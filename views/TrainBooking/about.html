About us

Introduction

Egyptian National Railway is one of the largest economic institutions in Egypt and the Arab world, and is the largest in the area of transport services (passengers and goods). It's considered the backbone of passenger transport in Egypt, where the volume of railway passengers and goods transport is: Passengers transport: 500 million passengers annually (about 1.4 million passengers a day). Goods transport: 6 million tons annually.
