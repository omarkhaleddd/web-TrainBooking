  • Basics

  • What is the train ticketing service?

    This service allows citizens to make train ticket reservations and pay for them online.


    • This service allows citizens to make train ticket reservations and pay for them online.


  • Who can benefit from this service?

    Any citizen holding a visa or master card credit card can use this service. The number of users for this service has reached 3 million.


    • Any citizen holding a visa or master card credit card can use this service. The number of users for this service has reached 3 million.


  • How much does this service cost and how can I pay for it?

    Payment can be made by Visa or Master Card credit cards as well as through Fawri points of sale. The service is available online for a 4% markup on the original ticket price.


    • Payment can be made by Visa or Master Card credit cards as well as through Fawri points of sale. The service is available online for a 4% markup on the original ticket price.


  • When can I reserve a train ticket?

    You can make a reservation up to 48 hours before the scheduled trip but no less.
